Our Mission

Rooted in
Love & Purpose

The mission of The Greater First Baptist Church is to love God, to love others, and to magnify the name of Jesus Christ — in our community, in our city, and throughout the world.

V

Our Vision

Called to
Make Disciples

Our vision is to glorify Jesus Christ, make true disciples throughout all nations through missionary activity, minister the ordinances, edify believers, and do all that is sovereignly possible and biblically permissible to magnify His name.

✝

Meet Our Pastor

Spiritual Leadership
for Our Community

"Called to shepherd, teach, and grow a congregation rooted in the Word of God."

The Greater First Baptist Church is blessed to be led by Rev. Dr. Jonathan Z. Queen, who serves as our Senior Pastor. Pastor Queen provides spiritual leadership, biblical teaching, and pastoral care to our entire church family.

As Senior Pastor, Dr. Queen oversees the spiritual development of our members, leads worship services, offers counseling and guidance, and casts the vision for our church's mission to love God, love others, and magnify Jesus Christ.

Our Ministries

Growing Together,
One Life at a Time

At The Greater First Baptist Church, our ministries are sacred spaces where faith is formed, questions are welcomed, and purpose takes shape. From first steps to bold leaps of faith, we walk this journey together.

🧒

Children's Ministry

Nurturing young hearts through Christ-centered teaching, joyful worship, and authentic relationships from the earliest age.

🙋

Youth Ministry

A vibrant space for teenagers to explore their faith, discover their identity, and grow as disciples of Christ.

✨

Young Adults

Helping young adults discover who they are, whose they are, and how God can use them to make a difference.

🎵

Music & Worship

Lifting up praise and glorifying God through the gift of music, choir, and collective worship each Sunday.

Missions
